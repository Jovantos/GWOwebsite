About Us

Welcome to GOOD WORK ORGANIZATION (GWO). We are a non-governmental, non-political, and non-profit organization established by Young South Sudanese. Our mission is to develop South Sudan in education, water and sanitation, food security, health, and capacity-building initiatives. We aim to improve social welfare within South Sudan and beyond.

South Sudan faces challenges in security, health, education, and infrastructure. As a civil society organization, it is our duty to ensure steady development by addressing these challenges in communities; children, girls, women, youth, and adults.

Our Objectives

  • Humanitarian Development Programs

    To run/initiate humanitarian development programs in counties, Payams, Bomas, and other state organs, to eradicate poverty in all its forms among communities and subsections.

  • Provide Shelter and Support

    Provide accommodation for the homeless and reach out to the needy and vulnerable people in society to support them.

  • Emergency Relief and Support

    Provide emergency support to the vulnerable, including orphans and widows, to meet their urgent needs.

  • Engagement and Enlightenment

    Become a platform to engage and enlighten society to be active participants in community development.
